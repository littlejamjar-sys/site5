Overview

Individually Assessed

Assignments may involve investigative research, field observation, information gathering, or verification work. Each matter is approached methodically, with careful attention to discretion and accuracy.

Services are not offered as fixed packages. Each matter is assessed individually following a brief consultation to determine what investigative support would be appropriate.

Services

What Is Offered

Investigation

Investigative assignments often begin with structured information gathering. This may include background inquiries, verification of claims, or research relating to ongoing legal or personal matters.

Work is conducted with a focus on establishing reliable information that can assist clients in understanding a situation more clearly before taking further action.

Field Work

Certain matters require observation or verification conducted at a specific location. Field assignments may involve site visits, environmental observation, or confirming activity related to a case.

Work is carried out carefully and discreetly, with an emphasis on accurate documentation and practical reporting.

Research

Remote investigative work may involve open-source intelligence research, document review, or structured analysis of publicly available information.

This type of investigation can assist with identifying relevant background information, timelines, and connections that may support further investigative work.

Mobile Operations

Investigative work can be conducted from a mobile setup when assignments require travel or location flexibility. This allows research, communication, and documentation to continue while working across multiple locations.

Mobile capability provides practical flexibility for assignments that extend beyond a single area or require on-site investigative presence.
